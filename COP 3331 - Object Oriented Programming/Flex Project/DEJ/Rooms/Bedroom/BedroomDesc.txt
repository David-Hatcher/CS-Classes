In the center of the room is a bed with an overstuffed comforter. A top the nightstand next to the bed there is a purple [ diary ] with a book mark located at what is presumable the last entry in the diary. In the far left corner there is a wooden antique dresser. Across from the door there is a window with the curtains pulled, letting very little light from the outside in. There is a short dresser with a mirror on top, in front of the mirror are various make up products and some small trinkets. Taped to the mirror are pictures, you can tell these are pictures of Emily and friends, they all seem very happy.
